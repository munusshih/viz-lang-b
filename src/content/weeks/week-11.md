## Open Call

I’m sharing an open call from [Data Through Design (DxD)](https://datathroughdesign.com/) — an annual exhibition that showcases artworks creatively analyzing and interpreting data from New York City’s Open Data portal, I encourage you all to apply!

- There is no application fee
- Selected artists will receive a stipend
- Deadline: November 16
